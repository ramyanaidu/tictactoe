About

This is a unique solution to collect feedback, preference of a user specifically targeted to food chains. Users select choices appropriate to their taste and preference and the choice is depicted via a game.

How does it work?

The idea of this project is to make the process of giving feedback interesting.

  • Users login into their profile and answer a simple question and play a game.

  • Users can keep track of their progress or level in the games, Unlock achievements, play PvP.

  • Food chains can give discount coupons for achievements to keep user interested.
Primary goal is to collect user preference data to help the food chains improve their service. User specific data like location, age range, ethnicity can also be collected.

Going forward..

The collection of data can be improved.
  • Recommendations of games for user based on past history.
  • Further comments and discussions in a PvP can capture more feedback data.
  • User can choose category based on specific food commodity or based on a game.
Games can also be outsourced to some gaming company.
